Chinese Rice with Sauce

Ingredients

  • Boiled rice500gm
  • Salt to taste
  • Cabbage 1 cup
  • Capsicum 1
  • Carrot 1
  • Green onions 1
  • Oil 2 tbsp
  • Onion 1
  • Chopped garlic 1-2 cloves
  • Beaten egg 1
  • sauce 2 tbsp
  • Sugar 2 tbsp

Sauce Ingredients

  • Fried onion 1
  • Salt to taste
  • Tomato ketchup 1 -2 cup
  • Soya Sauce 2 tbsp
  • Black pepper 1 tsp
  • White pepper 1 tsp
  • Corn flour 2 tbsp

Directions

Fried Rice

  1. Boil rice with 1 tbsp salt.
  2. Cut all veges like cabbage, capsicum, carrot, green onion and normal onion>
  3. Heat oil in a pan, add chopped garlic, saute it, then add whisked egg in it and break egg it into small pieces,
  4. then add all veges, after 2-3 minutes add boiled rice.
  5. Now add salt, soy sauce and sugar; toss gently so everything mix. Simmer on slow heat for 8-10 minutes.
  6. Top with green onion and serve with sauce.

Sauce

  1. For Sauce: Heat oil in a pan, add cloves of chooped garlic and then turn it brown, then add all other ingredients except corn flour and boil it.
  2. Now mix corn flour in a little water and pour in sauce while stirring and cook until thick. Sauce is ready.
